# 🚀 Investmate Nexus

Investmate Nexus is a full-stack AI-powered investment platform that connects **investors with entrepreneurs**, enabling idea validation, secure communication, portfolio management, and smart investment recommendations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Investmate Nexus is designed to bridge the gap between **startup founders and investors** by providing a centralized platform where users can:

- Pitch and discover business ideas
- Get AI-based investment recommendations
- Communicate via messaging and video calls
- Manage investment portfolios
- Validate startup ideas before funding
- Perform secure transactions
